At OYEX, we believe in the power of sports to bring people together and transform lives. Our journey began with a simple idea: to make the joy of badminton more accessible to everyone.

Founded by a passionate sports enthusiast, OYEX was born out of frustration with the lack of convenient platforms to buy quality badminton gear and book courts. Our founder, a lifelong lover of the game, realized the challenges players faced in finding the right equipment and reserving courts hassle-free. Determined to change that, OYEX was created to bridge the gap between enthusiasts and their passion. What started as a small initiative to support local players has grown into a comprehensive platform where athletes of all levels can find premium rackets, book courts with ease, and discover resources to improve their game. At OYEX, we’re more than a business; we’re a community. We are committed to empowering players, fostering connections, and making badminton a lifestyle for everyone.

Welcome to OYEX—where your game begins.
